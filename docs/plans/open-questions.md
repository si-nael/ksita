# Open Questions

## World Selection First
- World selection is complete. Future questions should deepen the Hollow rather than reopen generic alternatives.

## Must Decide Before Prototyping
- Which pure 2D presentation lane is strongest: oblique top-down, side-view, or chapter-specific switching between the two?
- How much should the player be able to predict relational changes before they happen?
- Which first encounter type is the most immediately fun in under 30 seconds: crossing pressure, hostile witness pursuit, rescue carry, or wrong-home decision?
- What bodily signs show that someone is losing anchoring?
- What kinds of creatures can survive naturally in an achronal void?
- How centralized or fragmented is the Common Vigil across distant settlements?
- What illegal forms of private witness can exist outside Common Vigil sanction?
- How much local variation can the Five Turns have before settlements stop feeling part of the same world?
- Which Turn is most emotionally dangerous in ordinary daily life: Facing, Carrying, Knotting, Returning, or Veiling?
- What visual signatures distinguish different Keeping styles or schools?
- How publicly known is Afterkeep versus how hidden it pretends to be?
- Do the starting Focus values preserve urgency, or do they make route reading too safe?
- Does Quick-Step feel best as the current purely positional correction tool, or does it still need a cadence interaction later?

## Must Decide Before Vertical Slice Lock
- How many world-law exceptions can exist before the Hollow stops feeling coherent?
- What is the target session length for one location-scale contradiction?
- How punishing should misarrival and drift be?
- How many simultaneous witness sources can a player reasonably parse without losing readability?
- What forms of hostile witness are interesting without becoming generic stealth enemies?
- Should clarity mode be available from the beginning or only as an accessibility option?
- How subtle can Held Wrong be before it becomes unfair?
- How easily can the Common Vigil revoke or repair public reality around a person or household?
- What does a false Returning look like in playable and emotional terms?
- How often should failure advance the area state instead of reloading immediately?
- How much of an Unkept person can remain lovable before the state stops being tragic and becomes cheap?
- What is the safest limit for pair-drift before it becomes unreadable in play?
- Which role in the Returning cluster should become the player's first obsession point?
- Which two roles in the Returning cluster create the strongest first pairing pressure?
- Which one of Iri, Nao, Yun, or Sera should become the first major non-POV obsession point opposite Lune?

## Strategic Questions
- Is the intended scale solo-dev, very small team, or longer multi-year production?
- Which mix of route divergence, optional pair content, and mastery challenges is the safest path to a 60-to-80-hour completionist target?
- What is the minimum shippable story length if the full completionist target proves too large for first release?
- Which gameplay pillar is the one feature that most strongly proves this is not a visual novel?
- Which progression axis feels best in play first: authority fluency, role licenses, or bond techniques?
- How early should the player gain a second route instrument after the Relay Lantern?
- Which wrong-home resolution feels strongest in play: lawful return, wrong home, or broken middle?
- Should the game prioritize Korean first, or bilingual release planning from the start?
- What is the acceptable balance between subtlety and clarity for story delivery?
- How much horror imagery is desirable versus emotional unease?
- How mythically dense should the prose and naming be before readability starts to suffer?
- How abstract can world explanation get before the player loses tactile understanding?

## Originality Checks
- Which place or ritual best proves this cannot be mistaken for an Earth-like fantasy world?
- What mechanics belong uniquely to time-independent physics rather than general surrealism?
- Which iconic image of the Hollow would remain memorable even without any plot context?
- Which traversal situation would be impossible to confuse with ordinary puzzle-platforming?
- Which witness cue family feels most iconic: bells, lanterns, statues, voices, or something stranger?
- Which Sequence role has the strongest favorite-character potential right now?
- Which Keeping image feels most uniquely ours: stolen halos, shadow seams, backcalls, or afterkept presence?

## Relationship Checks
- What does each core character need from the protagonist that they should not be allowed to need?
- Which relationship in Act 1 becomes more upsetting after its warmest scene?
- Which bond can never be resolved cleanly without betraying the project's identity?
- Which future bond becomes most dangerous because the Common Vigil decides who may publicly hold whom true?
- Which future bond becomes most dangerous because one side would rather be kept wrongly than returned correctly?

## Design Rule While Questions Remain Open
If a choice does not need to be fixed for the first playable proof, leave it open and test the emotional effect first.
