# Playtime And Scope

## Target
- The project should target approximately 20 to 30 hours for a first committed clear.
- Full completion, including achievements, optional routes, mastery content, and replay divergence, may target approximately 60 to 80 hours.
- The project should not target 80 hours of single-route linear story.

## Why This Structure
- The Hollow's strength is density, not raw volume. Stretching the main route to 80 hours would likely dilute the world's strongest scenes, roles, and relationship wounds.
- Completionist time can run longer because this setting naturally supports reinterpretation, risky revisits, illegal witness branches, and role-based mastery.

## Required Content Pillars

### 1. Main Route
- 6 to 8 major arcs.
- Approximately 3 to 4 hours each on first clear.
- Total target: 20 to 30 hours.
- The route should stay gameplay-forward; long passive story stretches should be the exception, not the default shape.

### 2. Character Obsession Layer
- Optional pair routes, side vows, aftermath scenes, and illegal witness branches.
- Total target: 10 to 15 hours.
- These routes should include mechanically distinct crossings or consequence states, not only extra dialogue.

### 3. System Mastery Layer
- Witness challenge routes, false Returning cases, drift recovery, harder Carrying sequences, and achievement-linked tasks.
- Total target: 10 to 15 hours.
- This layer should be one of the main reasons the project can justify an innovative identity.

### 4. Replay And Divergence Layer
- Postgame, ending variation, role-based viewpoint shifts, and public-versus-private witness consequences that cannot all be seen in one run.
- Total target: 10 to 20 hours.

## Production Rule
- Do not promise the 60-to-80-hour target until one full chapter template is proven fun, readable, and replay-worthy.
- If the first arc only works once, the project should be scoped downward rather than padded outward.

## Anti-Filler Rule
- Additional hours must come from reinterpretation, mastery, or divergence.
- If a planned section adds time without changing relationships, world law, or player skill, cut or merge it.
- If a planned section is mostly readable as a visual novel branch with light navigation, it does not count as meaningful playtime.

## Production Reality
- A 60-to-80-hour completionist target is feasible.
- For a solo developer or very small team, it is still a large multi-year scope and should be staged behind a smaller shippable core.
- An 80-hour main story is not recommended for this project's strengths.
