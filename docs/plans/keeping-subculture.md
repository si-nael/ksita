# Keeping Subculture

## Purpose
Define the look, strata, and scene energy of illegal witness so it feels like an actual subculture people want to dive into, not just a forbidden mechanic.

## Core Idea
Keeping is not one organization. It is a scene.

Some people fall into it through grief.
Some through romance.
Some through obsession.
Some because legal witness was never going to save what mattered to them.

The Hollow should make Keeping feel dangerous, glamorous, intimate, and socially infectious.

## Public Reputation
- beautiful ruin
- wrong home addicts
- halo thieves and room liars
- people who would rather be chosen badly than recognized correctly

## Style Split

### Common Vigil
- clean alignment
- pale veils
- balanced bell geometry
- sanctioned light
- symmetrical witness language

### Keeping
- asymmetry
- personal alteration
- stolen or cracked witness hardware
- shadow seams, hidden knot cords, wrong-colored light
- clothing and props that look customized around one person, not one institution

## The Three Visible Currents

### 1. Parlour Keeps

#### Energy
Domestic, intimate, obsessive, and deceptively soft.

#### What They Do
- pair knots
- hidden room holds
- wrong-home sleep arrangements
- quiet backcalls meant never to reach public space

#### Visual Feel
- undersleeve cords
- bedframe sigils
- private knot jewelry
- soft fabrics over dangerous relation marks
- hidden mirrored tokens worn under the collar or near the wrist

#### Appeal
This is where the shipping and private-ritual fascination hits hardest.

### 2. Span Breakers

#### Energy
Flashy, mobile, criminal, and hot-blooded.

#### What They Do
- false halo runs
- route theft
- cross-blind escort work
- emergency Keeping during failed Returns

#### Visual Feel
- short asymmetrical coats or route-cut silhouettes
- harnessed lantern mods
- cracked halo fragments worn openly or half-hidden
- gloves, boots, and hems marked by route burns and shadow stitching

#### Appeal
This is the cool, dangerous side of Keeping: charismatic, visibly illegal, and easy to fixate on.

### 3. After Houses

#### Energy
Tender, frightening, grief-soaked, and almost too quiet.

#### What They Do
- afterkeep
- memory-presence maintenance
- shadow residue tending
- keeping the nearly dead from being fully released

#### Visual Feel
- sleep-white cloth over dark seams
- lanterns with low wrong warmth
- bedside witness charms
- dried flowers, ash tags, and soft face covers

#### Appeal
This is the most tragic and disturbing current. It should feel unbearable and impossible to stop looking at.

## Social Strata

### High House Keeps
Quiet, hidden, expensive, and scandalous. Powerful households practice Keeping while publicly condemning it.

### Dockside Keeps
Practical, emotional, and mutual-aid adjacent. Common among carriers, crews, and route workers who know public systems fail real people constantly.

### Drift Edge Keeps
The most feared. These happen near blind wards, damaged routes, and near-Unkept spaces where relation law is already unstable.

## Style Rules
- sanctioned design is about role clarity; Keeping design is about person-specific distortion
- no two active keepers should look entirely standardized
- one visible sign should always imply a hidden second sign somewhere more intimate
- wrong light and altered shadows should matter as much as fabric and silhouette

## Signature Props
- cracked halo pieces
- personal backcall tokens
- shadow needles and seam clips
- two-person knot cords
- mirror sigils that only resolve when paired
- bed lanterns for wrong-home Veiling

## Character Pressure
Keeping should naturally produce:
- favorite duos
- morally bad but emotionally compelling choices
- design asymmetry people can recognize instantly
- people who look beautiful because they are already half-wrong

## Design Rule
If Keeping fashion and behavior feel like generic rebels in dark clothes, the subculture has failed.
