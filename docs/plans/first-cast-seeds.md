# First Cast Seeds

Status: working cast seed set. These are the first character candidates the Hollow currently wants to produce. Names can still change later, but their hooks and tensions should remain strong.

## Purpose
Move from abstract role families into the first set of people worth obsessing over.

These seeds exist to prove the world can generate characters, not just systems.

## The Five Seed Characters

## First Perspective Recommendation
Lune should be the first sustained point-of-view anchor.

Why:
- Lune can move through sanctioned and illegal spaces without flattening either side into lecture material
- Lune is active enough to carry gameplay and observant enough to carry world introduction
- Lune sits in the center of the returning conflict without reducing the others to satellites

### 1. Iri | Return Singer

#### Surface Hook
The most beautiful lawful voice in the Vigil Span, and the one person whose call fails exactly where it matters.

#### Signature Look
- pale throat-wrap with witness script at the collarbone
- three suspended bell rings behind the neck like a broken halo that aligns only while singing
- immaculate silhouette ruined by one always-untied cord

#### Why Diggable
Iri is easy to admire from far away and easy to doubt up close.

#### Private Damage
There is one person Iri does not want to return lawfully. Iri wants them to choose the lawful call for personal reasons, which already corrupts the role.

### 2. Nao | Backcaller

#### Surface Hook
The illegal voice that became someone's wrong home and never apologized for it.

#### Signature Look
- cracked halo shards hidden in dark hair or tied under the jaw
- one sleeve lined with shadow-stitching that moves before the hand does
- wrong-colored lantern light that always trails a half-step behind

#### Why Diggable
Nao reads first as dangerous, then as sincere, then as dangerous again for a different reason.

#### Private Damage
Nao may truly love the person being kept, but that love has already crossed into possession by changing what "home" means for them.

### 3. Yun | Twice-Called

#### Surface Hook
The person with two homes in their body and no stable way to survive both.

#### Signature Look
- double-shadow behavior, with one shadow answering late
- route marks that brighten under one call and darken under the other
- one eye, sleeve, or hemline subtly reacting to the wrong side of the bridge first

#### Why Diggable
Yun is not just a target of conflict. Yun is the place where the conflict becomes visible.

#### Private Damage
Part of Yun wants the wrong home because the wrong home actually knows something true.

### 4. Lune | Lantern Runner

#### Surface Hook
The route carrier holding everything together while quietly deciding which truth deserves to arrive.

#### Signature Look
- black-gold harness for carrying a witness lantern close to the ribs
- route-scored boots and gloved hands that show where the world has already burned them
- a bright, fast silhouette with tired eyes and no private stillness

#### Why Diggable
Lune looks like the cool intermediary role, but is actually the one who can make or break all three other people.

#### Private Damage
Lune is sick of being trusted as neutral. Neutrality in the Hollow is just delayed betrayal.

### 5. Sera | Turner

#### Surface Hook
The white-faced law who can make you unreal and is already too late to stay clean.

#### Signature Look
- severe pale veil geometry with almost no ornament except the judgment seals
- silence-bells that ring only after a decision has already landed
- route hooks and seal strips worn like execution tools

#### Why Diggable
Sera should feel frightening, composed, and one personal mistake away from being consumed by it.

#### Private Damage
Sera has already spared someone once or is about to. Either way, the role is cracked.

## The Pairs With Immediate Pull
- Iri and Nao: lawful home versus chosen home
- Nao and Yun: rescue, possession, devotion, or all three
- Lune and Yun: the one who keeps the road true and the one the road keeps breaking for
- Iri and Sera: mercy under public pressure
- Sera and Nao: two people who understand falsified reality better than anyone else and hate why

## First Iconic Conflict Image
Lock this image as the first strong promotional and tonal reference point:

Iri and Nao stand on opposite witness piers over the black underside sea, both calling Yun home.

The bridge between them is Held True only because Lune is carrying the route in the middle.
Yun's body faces Iri.
Yun's shadow moves toward Nao first.
Sera arrives after the call has already done the damage.

## Why This Seed Set Works
- every role has a clean silhouette and title hook
- every bond is already dangerous before plot specifics exist
- the world law is visible in the characters instead of only in the glossary
- this set invites favorites, pair debates, betrayal theories, and painful scenes immediately

## Design Rule
If future revisions make these characters more sensible but less magnetic, the revision is wrong.
