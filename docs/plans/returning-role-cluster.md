# Returning Role Cluster

Status: baseline role cluster locked for future cast design. These are not final named characters, but they define the first magnetic conflict shape the project should build from.

## Cluster Purpose
This is the first role bundle the Hollow should be able to generate before a full story is written.

It exists to prove that the world can create character obsession, visual contrast, and dangerous pair dynamics on its own.

## Core Conflict Image
At Returning, two voices call the same person across the Vigil Span.

One call is public, lawful, and world-stable.
One call is private, illegal, and emotionally irresistible.

A route holds just long enough for everyone to make it worse.

## The Five Roles

### 1. Return Singer

#### Surface Hook
The sanctioned voice who can call most people home, but fails hardest when it matters most.

#### Signature Look
- throat bells or layered voice seals
- a pale mantle cut to broadcast sound and status
- witness script around the mouth, collar, or neck

#### Public Role
Lawful home-caller during Returning.

#### Private Contradiction
They want one person to come back for a reason that is not lawful, neutral, or purely merciful.

#### Scene Promise
A public Return that sounds flawless until one name refuses to belong to it.

### 2. Backcaller

#### Surface Hook
The illegal voice that has become someone's wrong home.

#### Signature Look
- cracked or stolen halo fragments
- off-register bells, mismatched cords, or throat scars
- shadow seams that move when they speak a private call

#### Public Role
Criminal, corrupter, route-contaminant.

#### Private Contradiction
They may be more sincere than the law allows and more possessive than love should survive.

#### Scene Promise
A whisper-level call that overrides a whole lawful chorus.

### 3. The Twice-Called

#### Surface Hook
The person who now has two homes and may survive neither.

#### Signature Look
- split witness marks or double-shadow behavior
- asymmetrical route response when they move
- clothing or body details that answer to two different relation systems

#### Public Role
Instability risk, disputed return subject, someone people talk about in lowered voices.

#### Private Contradiction
They may genuinely want both calls, neither call, or the wrong one.

#### Scene Promise
Standing still while the world tears its routes toward them from two sides.

### 4. Lantern Runner

#### Surface Hook
The glamorous route carrier trapped in the middle because the crossing only holds if they do.

#### Signature Look
- black-gold lantern harness
- void-burned boots or route-scored sleeves
- light lines that show where they last carried witness

#### Public Role
Trusted mover of witness through dangerous crossings.

#### Private Contradiction
They are supposed to deliver reality cleanly, but they may be secretly helping one side cheat it.

#### Scene Promise
Holding a bridge true for people who should not all be allowed on it together.

### 5. Turner

#### Surface Hook
The official who can decide which call gets to remain real.

#### Signature Look
- severe veil geometry
- silence-heavy bells that ring only when judgment lands
- route hooks, seal strips, or withdrawal marks carried like execution tools

#### Public Role
The one who can correct, suspend, or end disputed witness.

#### Private Contradiction
They cannot bear the thought of making the wrong person unreal, which means they may already have.

#### Scene Promise
Arriving after the emotional point of no return and making the room worse simply by being the law.

## Pair Dynamics To Preserve
- Return Singer and Backcaller: lawful home versus chosen home
- Backcaller and Twice-Called: rescue or possession, depending on the scene
- Return Singer and Turner: public mercy against public necessity
- Lantern Runner and Twice-Called: the person holding the road and the person the road keeps bending toward
- Turner and Backcaller: mutual recognition between two people who both know how reality is falsified

## Why This Cluster Works
- each role teaches a Hollow law without becoming an exposition puppet
- every role has immediate silhouette and title appeal
- the conflict is interpersonal first, but still inseparable from world law
- it creates natural fan-favorite possibilities without needing a full plot outline first

## Visual Split

### Sanctioned Side
- clean bells
- aligned halos
- pale witness fabrics
- stable route-light and crisp silhouettes

### Illegal Side
- cracked halos
- off-register metal
- stitched shadows
- too-personal lantern light and asymmetrical marks

## Design Rule
If this cluster ever feels like a generic triangle plus an authority figure, it has failed. The tension must come from route law, home-calling, and the right to keep someone real.
